Got my early education at "Sekolah Kebangsaan Tebuk Pancur" (SKTP). It is located in Simpang Empat, Semanggol, Perak. This is the first place where I got my formal education at an early age.

This is the second place I got my formal education which is "Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Gunung Semanggol" (SMKGS). This is the place where I gain new experience and knowledge. The most interesting part of this school is that it is located at mountain slope which I guess all of you know how adventure it is. However, this is the place that I miss the most.

As time passed, I further my studies at Perak Matriculation College, also known as "Kolej Matrikulasi Perak" (KMPk) for about a year. I continued my studies in Biological Science moduled three. To be truth, this was the tough and challenging phase for me as a student.

As I finished my study at Perak Matriculation College, I continued my studies at "Universiti Teknologi Mara" (UiTM). I further my bachelor's degree study in Library and Information Management.
